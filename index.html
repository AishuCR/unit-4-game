GAME OF THRONES!


Game Instructions:


White walker's score will be automatically generated at the start of the game.


There are four team members on your side. By clicking on each of them you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to White walker's score, you lose the game if your score goes above the White walker's score.


The value of each team member is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each team members.
